## 📋 Problem Statement
Micro-businesses in India (jewelry stores, coaching institutes, real estate agents) often struggle with expensive and complex CRM systems like Salesforce or Zoho. They manage 90% of their business via WhatsApp but lose track of leads, payments, and follow-ups in cluttered chat histories.

## 💡 Solution
**SyncSetu** acts as an intelligent, aesthetically premium layer on top of WhatsApp. It organizes contacts, tracks deal values, and manages tasks quietly and beautifully without the complexity of traditional CRMs.

---

## ✨ Key Features
*   **WhatsApp OTP Login**: Secure, industry-standard authentication using WhatsApp business numbers.
*   **Premium Dashboard**: Real-time widgets for New Leads, Conversion Rates, and Revenue tracking.
*   **Contact Management (CRUD)**: Full control over your client database with advanced status mapping (Lead, Active, Negotiation, Closed).
*   **Visual Sales Pipeline**: Kanban-style view to move leads through the sales lifecycle.
*   **Task & Follow-up System**: Never miss a client meeting with dedicated overdue and upcoming task cards.
*   **Payment Tracking**: Log and monitor pending payments with ₹ currency support.
*   **SEO Optimized**: High-performance rendering with full meta-tag support for search visibility.

---

## 💻 Tech Stack
*   **Frontend**: React 18, Vite, Redux Toolkit, Framer Motion (Animations), Lucide Icons.
*   **Backend**: Node.js, Express.js.
*   **Database**: MongoDB Atlas (Cloud).
*   **Styling**: Vanilla CSS3 (Premium Glassmorphism Design System).
*   **Deployment**: Vercel (Edge Functions).
